post in streetwear reddits and talk to clothing making youtubers

Website that allows users to find local or distanced people that can sew, embroider, knit, screenprint etc an item for them. 
People with an embroidery machine or sewing machine can set up their page that shows their work and services. 

Use case:

    I need a shirt made for my daughter with "Lastname" embroidered on it. I search for local people that can do this for me and send them 
    a message. We agree to meet and provide the shirt. I get it emrboided and payment is confirmed.
